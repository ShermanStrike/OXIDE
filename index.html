    WELCOME TO OXIDE

    OXIDE is an international clan whose primary focus is to activity partake in both strongholds and clan wars. We are currently focusing on tier VIII battles, although we are looking to expand our playerbase in order to more effectivly fight on the global map in tier X.

    The clan first started as a dutch clan, made by a group of players who had fought together for years. It was decided in early 2017 that the clan were to go international in an attempt to expand their playerbase and to try to fullfill their goals, conquering the global map.

    Currently in the top 500 clans, OXIDE has silently climbed the leaderboards, but we are now looking to create a name for ourselfes - Locked and Loaded

    If you are looking to join, please visit our recruitment page.
